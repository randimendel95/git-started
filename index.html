Bambi

Bambi is the main character of the movie Bambi, which depicts the fictional life of a young fawn. In this story, Bambi grows up to take his father's title as Great Prince of the Forest. His mother, who he is close with, is killed, which causes him much grief. As he becomes grown, he falls in love with a female named Faline, and saves her from a forest fire, and flees to shelter, having twins the next spring.

Bambi appears in the Walt Disney movie Bambi
